How Alcojón works?

The Alcojón has a kit and a seperate mobile app

Step 1: User input

Person enters manually his age, weight, height and gender.

Step 2: Breath analyze

Person blows into a built-in breathalyzer to check his blood alcohol concentration (BAC). Estimated BAC is shown on the display.

Step 3: Calculation

Based on BAC + manually input data the know-how kit calculates:

  • amount of ethanol in your body
  • how much time your liver needs to process the alcohol
  • how much water is needed to balance the body
  • how many hours of sleep/detox is needed for a body recovery

Step 4: Hologramic output

User can choose one of the body organs to be displayed as a hologram in the creative part of the kit. Choosing the body part (brain, heart, liver) by clicking the button - a hologram appears showing the organ with highlighted parts affected by alcohol consumption.

Step 5: Health tips

Mobile App suggests health tips based on user's data. If user sets goals, he automatically participates in the reward system giving discount vouchers to alternative entertainment services

Further information

With our interactive know-how kit, we promote smart and conscious alcohol consumption with contextual indicators for a better understanding of alcohol influence on a body. The idea is that if you start identifying your drinking patterns then you can master, minimize or tolerate triggers of stress and conflict by effective coping strategies.

Publicly accessible real-time intoxication tests help to understand the alcohol influence on a human body in a specific moment based on a breath sample. Once your blood alcohol content (BAC) is estimated, then you can see how alcohol affects your body. Additionally, the test acts as a prevention tool for obtaining basic information about estimations on the body recovery steps.

Through this voluntary check we shift the focus of a human brain’s rewarding system into a positive flow of self-awareness or “treat yourself good” attitude. With a software add-on (eg Native Web App) a person can track total consumption patterns and based on this analysis set up harm reduction goals. Fulfillment of these goals can be exchanged for a discount voucher of an alternative type of entertainment.
